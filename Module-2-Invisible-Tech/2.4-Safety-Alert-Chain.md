# 2.4 Safety Alert Chain: Council Consensus

## Executive Summary
The Council has defined a multi-tiered, psychologically-aware safety alert system that balances immediate technical responsiveness with the emotional stability of the child and the regulatory requirements of PMI compliance.

## Council Perspectives

### Nexus (Hardware/IoT Architect)
**Proposal:** A zero-latency, redundant trigger system using mmWave and seat sensors. 
- **Infrastructure:** Local MQTT broker on a Home-Lab server to ensure offline functionality.
- **Connectivity:** PoE (Power over Ethernet) for primary sensors to eliminate battery-related failures.
- **Tiers:** 
    - Level 1: Soft warnings (positional shifts).
    - Level 2: Haptic vibration for caregiver + dashboard notification.
    - Level 3: Critical alarms (audible + visual).

### Lumina (Child Psychologist/EJE)
**Critique:** Warned against "panic-inducing" alerts. High-decibel alarms and abrupt lighting shifts create cortisol spikes in infants, which can damage the sense of security and trigger separation anxiety.
- **Requirement:** "Gentle Escalation." The system must prioritize discrete notifications for the adult before moving to ambient signals that the child might perceive.

### Sora (Experience Designer)
**Refinement:** Rejected "hospital-white" emergency lighting in favor of "Chromatic Warning Tones."
- **Visual Language:** 
    - Level 1: Soft pulsing amber in peripheral lighting.
    - Level 2: Saturated gold glow.
    - Level 3: High-contrast flashing patterns.
- **Experience:** Lighting should act as a directional guide, subtly leading the caregiver toward the child.

### Atlas (Strategic Realist/Compliance)
**Operational Mandate:** Focus on liability, auditability, and the "human failure" variable.
- **Compliance:** Every alert and its resolution must be timestamped and logged (locally and off-site) for PMI audit trails.
- **Fail-safe:** Implementation of a "Dead Man's Switch"—automatic escalation to the supervisor/Angel Parent if the primary caregiver does not acknowledge a Level 2 alert within 15 seconds.

## Final Consensus Conclusion: The Tiered Gentle-Escalation Chain

The Symbiotic Nest will implement a **Tiered Gentle-Escalation Alert Chain** based on the following specifications:

1. **The Technical Core:** A PoE-powered sensor array reporting to a local MQTT broker, with a mirrored cloud log for regulatory compliance.
2. **The Escalation Logic:**
    - **Phase 1 (Discrete):** Wearable haptic nudge (Vibration) $\rightarrow$ Primary Caregiver.
    - **Phase 2 (Ambient):** Pulse Amber $\rightarrow$ Gold lighting transitions + Dashboard notification.
    - **Phase 3 (Critical):** High-contrast visual patterns + Targeted audio cue + Immediate escalation to secondary supervisor.
3. **The Safety Window:** A strict 15-second window for acknowledgment of Phase 2 alerts before automatic Phase 3 escalation.
4. **The Emotional Guardrail:** All audio and visual signals are designed to be "calming yet insistent," avoiding the sirens-and-lights approach to maintain the nursery's sanctuary atmosphere.
