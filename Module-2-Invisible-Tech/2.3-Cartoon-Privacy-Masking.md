# 2.3 Cartoon Privacy Masking

## Council Discussion Summary

### Nexus (Hardware/IoT Architect)
**Proposal:** Implementation of a real-time Pose Estimation pipeline (MediaPipe/YOLOv8-pose) on edge hardware (Jetson Orin Nano). The system extracts skeletal data and maps it to a 3D mesh, ensuring raw biometric data never leaves the local network. 
**Technical Focus:** Latency reduction and local-first processing.

### Lumina (Child Psychologist/EJE)
**Critique:** Warned against the 'Mirror Effect' and emotional distancing. Argued that over-sanitized cartoon avatars could strip away vital non-verbal cues (the 'imperfections' of movement) that parents rely on for emotional bonding and understanding the child's state.
**Psychological Focus:** Preservation of emotional valence and kinetic authenticity.

### Sora (Experience Designer)
**Refinement:** Proposed 'Atmospheric Abstraction' instead of literal cartoons. Suggested using soft, glowing, amorphous shapes or watercolor silhouettes that shift colors based on the child's mood (integrated with the Oscar Moments algorithm), transforming surveillance into a piece of living art.
**Aesthetic Focus:** Removing the 'uncanny valley' and enhancing the spiritual connection.

### Atlas (Strategic Realist/Compliance)
**Constraint:** Noted that 'glowing shapes' are insufficient for PMI (Private Micro-crèche) legal compliance and safety audits. Proposed a 'Dual-Stream Audit Trail': a privacy-preserving stream for parents and a highly encrypted, local-only 'Break-Glass' raw feed for emergencies.
**Operational Focus:** Regulatory compliance, immutable logging, and redundant encrypted storage.

---

## Final Consensus Conclusion

The Council has reached a consensus to implement **Hybrid Atmospheric Masking with a Break-Glass Audit Stream**.

1. **Visual Representation:** Use edge-based pose estimation to drive a stylized, non-literal visual representation (Atmospheric Abstraction) for remote parental viewing. This ensures that raw biometric data is not streamed.
2. **Emotional Integrity:** The visualization will be designed to preserve the kinetic authenticity and emotional valence of the child's movements, ensuring parents remain connected to the child's true state.
3. **Compliance & Safety:** A secondary, high-resolution raw feed will be archived locally. Access to this stream is strictly governed by a 'Break-Glass' protocol requiring multi-party authentication (Director + Parent) and producing immutable access logs to satisfy PMI regulatory requirements.
4. **Hardware:** Deployment on Jetson Orin Nano for edge processing to minimize latency and maximize privacy.
