# 2.2 Oscar Moments Algorithm: Council Consensus

## Overview
The 'Oscar Moments Algorithm' is designed to identify and capture peak emotional and developmental experiences ('Oscar Moments') of the child, providing parents with meaningful highlights without overwhelming them with constant surveillance data.

## The Council Debate

### Nexus (Hardware/IoT Architect)
**Proposal:** A 'Hype-Trigger' architecture.
- Use mmWave radar to detect sudden acceleration or specific gesture patterns (jumping, waving).
- Audio frequency analysis to detect 'peak laughter' or 'surprise vocalizations'.
- Trigger high-fidelity video snippets (5s pre- and post-event).
- **Goal:** Technical efficiency and high-signal capture.

### Lumina (Child Psychologist/EJE)
**Critique:** Danger of 'Peak Bias'.
- High-energy moments aren't the only valuable ones. 'Quiet Oscars' (the first time a baby recognizes a parent's voice or a moment of deep focus) are equally critical.
- Proposed adding 'State-Change' detection: transitions from distress to calm or solitude to social engagement.
- **Goal:** Emotional balanced representation of the child's development.

### Sora (Experience Designer)
**Refinement:** From 'Alert' to 'Atmosphere'.
- Real-time notification is too intrusive. Proposed 'Atmospheric Echoes': subtle lighting shifts (e.g., warm amber) in the parent's workspace to signal a moment is happening.
- Asynchronous delivery via a 'Curated Digital Scrapbook' rather than a raw event stream.
- **Goal:** Emotional resonance and aesthetic integration.

### Atlas (Strategic Realist/Compliance)
**Constraint:** Privacy and Viability.
- Strict adherence to PMI regulations and privacy laws.
- All processing must be local; no raw video clips uploaded to the cloud.
- Implementation of 'Safe Zones' where recording is permitted.
- Capping high-fidelity captures (e.g., 5 per day) to prevent SSD wear and parent 'alert fatigue'.
- **Goal:** Legal compliance and operational sustainability.

## Final Consensus Conclusion
The Council adopts the **Hybrid State-Peak Algorithm**.

1. **Detection Logic:**
   - **Peak Trigger:** mmWave + Audio for high-energy events.
   - **State Trigger:** Behavioral state-change detection for 'Quiet Oscars'.
2. **User Experience:**
   - **Real-time:** Subtle lighting cues ('Atmospheric Echoes') for the parent.
   - **Review:** Daily curated 'Highlight Reel' in a digital scrapbook.
3. **Governance:**
   - **Local-First:** Local encryption and processing.
   - **Spatial Bounds:** Restricted to 'Safe Zones'.
   - **Quota:** Daily limit on high-fidelity clips to ensure system longevity and cognitive load management.
